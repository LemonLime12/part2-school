School church

Was established in 1978

A church-exempt school operates its educational program in accordance with standards that are established by the governing church.



school library

Was built in 1980

A school library is a library within a school where students, staff, and often, parents of a public or private school have access to a variety of resources.



school auditorium

The auditorium as a unit in school buildings seems to have been. introduced to provide a place for the assembly of pupils and teachers. for opening exercises and announcements. During most of the day. the auditorium either was idle or was used as a study hall.
